NERDY NUMMIES

Let's Bake Some Sweet Treats!

Nerdy Nummies is a Youtube channel I loved to watch when I was a kid. My mom loves to bake, so I'd always search up recipes and how-to videos of pastries and baked goods I wanted to make.This rainbow cake video is a tutorial I remember watching back over and over again, because the cake looked so yummy and vibrant. I also really enjoyed the quirkiness of the Youtuber, Rosanna Pansino and how she really made me feel like everything she made was easy to bake and something I could make too! I never got around to following any of her actual recipes but I loved her videos!
